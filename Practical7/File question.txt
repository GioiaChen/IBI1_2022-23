Question: 
What is the trend of the death rate of covid in Austria?

The question is addressed in the covid.py file from line to line.

Answer:
The death rate can be indicated from the proportion of total deaths and total cases. 
According to the plot, the proportion of total deaths continuously dropped since the number of total cases increased rapidly and the number of total deaths grew at a very slow speed.
This means that the deeath rate of covid in Austria has a trend of decreasing.
